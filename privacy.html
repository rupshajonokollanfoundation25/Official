Privacy & Terms of Use Policy

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

Information Collection

At Rupsa Jonokolyan Foundation (RJ Foundation), we collect your name, email, and profile picture (if provided) on our website. This data is primarily required for Firebase Authentication and the smooth operation of the website's review section.

Use of Information

The collected information is used strictly for verifying your profile and securely displaying your reviews on our website. We absolutely do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third-party organizations.

Data Security

Your details are safely stored within the Google Firebase Cloud database. It incorporates modern cryptographic encryption protocols to ensure that unauthorized individuals cannot access your private data.

Donation Policy

Any contribution made to our foundation is spent entirely on social welfare activities. Please ensure you double-check and accurately input your Transaction ID and mobile number when donating. A confirmation text message or email may be triggered upon successful transactions.

For any accidental transactions or refund requests, please contact us strictly within 24 hours of your donation payment.

Comment & Review Policy

You reserve the right to state your thoughts in our review section; however, the following actions remain strictly forbidden:

  • Using obscene, offensive, or derogatory language.
  • Posting political or religious hate-filled remarks.
  • Defamatory or malicious attacks targeting any individual or organization.

The foundation management retains full authority to eliminate any review breaching these policies without issuing any prior explanation.

Cookies & External Links

To deliver an optimized user experience and persist your active login session, our site may utilize temporary browser cookies. Furthermore, our web application might house hyperlinks redirection towards our Facebook Group or YouTube channel—upon interacting with them, their respective platform guidelines will apply.
