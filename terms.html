Membership Terms & Conditions

Voluntary Mindset

One must possess a dedicated mindset to selflessly and voluntarily engage in the humanitarian and social welfare activities of the Rupsha Jonokollan Foundation.

Collaborative Behavior

Always maintain a highly respectful, professional, and cooperative relationship with all members, executives, and the general public for the greater interest of the foundation.

Monthly Contribution

To keep organizational operational activities smooth and active, the fixed monthly contribution determined by the foundation must be paid regularly between the 1st and 15th of every month.

Constitution & Discipline

All official rules, organizational regulations, the framework constitution, and executive decisions made by the committee must be strictly and respectfully followed.

Regular Meeting Presence

Members should prioritize attending the monthly or emergency sessions organized by the foundation and proactively deliver constructive feedback for continuous development.

Non-Political Stance

This is strictly a non-political and non-profit welfare institution. The official title, designation, or name of the organization must never be exploited for political or personal gain.
